ABOUT ME my career path

My name is Mona Es-Said. I graduated with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Marketing and an Asian and Pacific Studies minor in December 2017. Over the course of my degree and afterwards, I worked in human resources, marketing, and nonprofits.

My ultimate goal for my career has always been to make a positive difference in peoples' lives. Each of the positions I have held have taught me new skills and given me a better understanding of myself and how I would like to make an impact.

I eventually realized that I wanted to shift towards more technology-focused roles, so I decided to continue my education in order to do that. I enrolled at the University of Glasgow and am currently working towards my Master's of Science in Software Development with an anticipated graduation date in early December 2023.

I am looking forward to starting the next stage of my career as a Software Developer!
